How Does It Work?

Promoters pay to show an NFT when users open a new browser tab.

Promoters get exposure; users get to explore NFTs and keep a portion of the promo payments.

Pick a Date

Pick a date for your promotion. Each promot runs for 24 hours from 12:00 UTC to all users who have installed the browser extension.

Deposit USDC

Promoters deposit $1,000 USDC into a smart contract, which enables the campaign to run on the chosen date.

Users View Promo

When Users open a tab, they see the promoted NFT. They may continue browsing or click on the call-to-action to learn more.

Users Get Paid

Users are eligible to get a share of the promo payments if they open browser tabs. The payments are split evenly among daily active users via the smart contract.
