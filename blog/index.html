HOW DOES IT WORK?

Advertisers pay to show you a webpage when you open a new tab.

They get exposure; you get to explore new pages and keep a portion of their payments.

Sign-Up

Start by installing our browser extension, and then connect your wallet to receive payments from promoters.

Promoters Pay

Advertisers want to share their products with the world, so they pay to showcase their webpage by paying AlphaBack.

Discover Products

When you open a tab, you see a promoted webpage. If there is no advertised page, then you’re current browser experience does not change!

Get Paid

You are eligible to get a share of ad payments when you open browser tabs. The payments are split evenly among daily active users.
